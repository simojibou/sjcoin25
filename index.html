How to Play Idle Drift & Merge

1. Buy basic cars using your idle currency (pineapple icon).

2. Place them in the grid to start earning passively (earn/sec).

3. Drag one car onto another identical level car to merge them into a higher level car, which earns more.

4. Use Boost for temporary earnings multiplier.

5. Toggle Auto Mode to automatically earn without manual collection (premium feature simulation).

6. Explore other modes like PVP, Road Trip for more rewards.

Connect your wallet for NFT claiming (not implemented yet).
